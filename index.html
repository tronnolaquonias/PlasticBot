VTHacks 2024 - PlasticBot

PlasticBot

How It Works

Step 1

The AI/ML model takes an input picture and identifies the pieces of plastic in the picture. For example in this picture there is a turtle swimming right next to a plastic bag.

Step 2

The image is processed through AI to identify the pieces of plastic underwater. The AI model had been trained with thousands of pictures of underwater plastic beforehand.

Step 3

The picture is returned with bounding boxes around the identified pieces of plastic with a confidence interval between 0 - 1, which shows the AI's level of confidence.

Potential Usage

This machine learning model could be used with autonomous underwater vehicles in order to automatically detect trash and collect it. This would help clean up the pollution in the ocean.
